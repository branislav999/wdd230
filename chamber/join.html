Membership Levels

Gold Membership

Gold Membership, priced at $500 annually, provides premium visibility on the Belgrade Chamber website, exclusive networking opportunities with key stakeholders, and priority consideration for Chamber-sponsored activities such as trade missions.

Silver Membership

Silver Membership, priced at $300 annually, includes a detailed business profile on the website, invitations to networking mixers and seminars, and access to discounted advertising rates in Chamber publications.

Bronze Membership

Bronze Membership, at $150 annually, offers a basic listing on the Chamber website, invitations to select events, and access to Chamber resources like business directories.

NP Membership

Non-Profit (NP) Membership, priced at $0 annually, grants non-profit organizations a platform on the Chamber website, invitations to networking events, and access to resources for fundraising and collaboration efforts.
